Congratulations to Prof. Sriraam Natarajan on being elected as a Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) fellow! He has been recognized for his significant contributions to the field of Artificial Intelligence, specifically Statistical Relational AI, healthcare applications, and his dedicated service to the AAAI community. This is a remarkable milestone and a well-deserved honor. 

Congratulations again, Professor! We are all incredibly proud of you.
