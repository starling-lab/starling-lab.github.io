Congratulations to Prof. Sriraam Natarajan on being elected as Councilor of the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI)! The AAAI Executive Council is the volunteer leadership of AAAI, and invests in advancing the field of AI and the AAAI association. Earlier this year, Prof. Natarajan was also elected as a Fellow of AAAI in recognition of his significant contributions to the field of AI, particularly in Statistical Relational AI, healthcare applications, and his dedicated service to the AAAI community.

Congratulations once again, Professor! Well deserved!
